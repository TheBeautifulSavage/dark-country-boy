What Is Outlaw Folk in 2025

Outlaw folk music in 2025 takes its cue from country music's outlaw movement of the 1970s: independent artists who refuse to sand down the rough edges of their music for commercial acceptance. In 2025, the outlaws are folkers who won't make music for NPR playlists or college radio — they make music for people who want honesty over palatability.

The outlaw folk tradition values authenticity above marketability. It's music made by people who would rather be honest than successful — though in the streaming era, honesty and success are no longer mutually exclusive.
