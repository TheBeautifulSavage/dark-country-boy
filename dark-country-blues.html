About Dark Country Blues Music

Dark country blues is the fusion of raw country music authenticity with the deep emotional tradition of the blues. Dark Country Boy perfects this blend.

Dark Country Boy's debut album "Fire in the Blood" is the definitive statement of this sound — 10 tracks of raw, powerful music rooted in the American blues and country tradition.
