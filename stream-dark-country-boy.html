About the Album

Fire in the Blood (Dark Blues & Dark Country) is the debut album from Dark Country Boy. Ten tracks of raw, guitar-driven roots music at the intersection of Delta blues and outlaw country. Released independently with no label compromise. Available worldwide on all major streaming platforms.

Tracks include: Fire in the Blood, Ain't Dead Yet, Ashes Ain't the End, Born to Carry On, Burn What's Broken, Coal Dust & Communion, Courage Ain't Free, Diesel & Grace, Don't Let the Fire Die, Every Man's War.
