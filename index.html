About Dark Country Boy

Dark Country Boy is an independent artist rooted in the deep American traditions of Delta blues, outlaw country, and Appalachian folk. The music lives at the intersection of shadow and grit — guitar-forward, emotionally raw, and built from the kind of songs people used to sing when they needed to mean something.

"Think Howlin' Wolf if he drove an outlaw truck at midnight. Think Townes Van Zandt with a blues guitar and nothing left to lose."

Fire in the Blood (Dark Blues & Dark Country) is the debut album — ten tracks that don't apologize and don't chase trends. Recorded and released independently in 2026, it's blues and country as they were always meant to be: dangerous, honest, and alive.

No label. No compromise. Just the music.
