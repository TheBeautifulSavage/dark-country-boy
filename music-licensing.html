FAQ

Common Questions

Are Dark Country Boy tracks royalty-free?

Licensing fees are flat-rate — you pay once and use the track for the project covered in your license. There are no ongoing royalties owed for YouTube monetization under a Creator or Commercial license.

Will my YouTube videos get Content ID claimed?

With a valid Creator or Commercial license, we will not claim your video. We can provide a YouTube Content ID whitelist clearance letter with your license to prevent false claims.

Can I use the music in a podcast?

Yes. The Creator license covers podcast use. Include the credit line "Music by Dark Country Boy (darkcountryboy.com)" in your show notes.

Do you offer exclusive licenses?

Yes, for broadcast and high-value commercial projects. Contact us to discuss exclusive use pricing — we can negotiate on a per-track basis.

What file formats do you deliver?

All licenses include high-quality MP3 (320kbps). WAV files (24-bit/44.1kHz or 48kHz) and stems (individual instrument tracks) are available on request for Commercial and Broadcast licenses.

How quickly can I get my license?

Usually within 24 hours. Once you email us, we'll confirm your license in writing and send the audio files. Rush licensing is available for urgent projects.

Can I request a custom edit or instrumental version?

Yes. Contact us and we can arrange custom edits, extended or shortened versions, and instrumental mixes for an additional fee.
