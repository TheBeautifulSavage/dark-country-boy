Artist Biography (Press Use)

Dark Country Boy is an independent roots music artist whose catalog spans the full breadth of American musical darkness — Delta blues, outlaw country, Southern Gothic, and Americana. With over 70 albums and more than 1,400 original songs, Dark Country Boy has built one of the most prolific and uncompromising bodies of work in independent music.

The musical DNA runs through the tradition of Johnny Cash, Waylon Jennings, Howlin' Wolf, and Robert Johnson — artists who understood that the truth in American music lives in the dark corners, not the bright ones. Songs about sin, violence, faith, survival, war, and the particular weight of living in a country still working out what it is. No apologies, no compromises, no radio edits.

The catalog spans every dimension of the dark country and dark blues traditions: Southern Gothic swamp albums (Swamp Ritual, Bayou Chains & Whiskey Flames), outlaw political songwriting (Whiskey, Waco & Washington, Blood for Oil, Outlaw Democracy), military and veteran blues (Outlaw Paratrooper Blues, Children of War), outlaw preacher mythology (Gospel of a Smuggler, Holy Outlaw), and pure Delta blues-rooted dark country (Fire in the Blood, Borrowed Time Blues, Demons of the Delta).

Dark Country Boy is available for streaming on all major platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, and iHeartRadio. The entire catalog is independently produced and released, maintaining complete artistic control.

Press Release

For Immediate Release

Dark Country Boy Releases Expansive Dark Country & Blues Catalog — 70+ Albums Now Streaming

Independent artist Dark Country Boy has assembled one of the largest independent catalogs in the dark country and blues genre, with over 70 albums and 1,400 original songs now available across all major streaming platforms.

Rooted in the tradition of Johnny Cash, Waylon Jennings, and Howlin' Wolf, Dark Country Boy's music occupies the intersection of Delta blues and outlaw country — raw, unfiltered, and deeply committed to the American roots music tradition. The catalog spans Southern Gothic storytelling, outlaw political commentary, war blues, swamp music, and spiritual outlaw mythology.

Select album titles from the catalog include: Fire in the Blood, Demons of the Delta, Outlaw Paratrooper Blues, Gospel of a Smuggler, Blood River Hymns, Whiskey, Waco & Washington, Swamp Ritual, Dead Men Don't Pray, Bayou Chains & Whiskey Flames, The Rusted Republic, Raised by Wolves, Buried with Sins, and Dark Americana: Country Blues — Gravel in My Blood, among many others.

The artist cites the outlaw ethos of artists who prioritized truth over commerce: "The tradition of American roots music at its most powerful has always been the tradition of people who had no reason to lie."
