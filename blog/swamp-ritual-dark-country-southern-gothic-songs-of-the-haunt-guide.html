Dark Country Boy: "Swamp Ritual: Dark Country & Southern Gothic Songs of the Haunted South"

Dark Country Boy · 35 Tracks · Dark Country & Blues

Dark Country Boy's album Swamp Ritual: Dark Country & Southern Gothic Songs of the Haunted South is 35 tracks deep in swamp blues and Southern Gothic atmosphere. Drawing from Creedence Clearwater Revival, early ZZ Top, and the swamp rock tradition, this record sits squarely in the dark country and American roots tradition that Dark Country Boy has been building across 70 albums.

The Sound

This album captures Dark Country Boy at the intersection of raw guitar-driven blues and outlaw country storytelling. The 35 tracks span the full emotional range of the genre — from fire and defiance to quiet mourning and hard-won survival.

If you're looking for dark country music, outlaw blues, or authentic American roots music that doesn't compromise, Swamp Ritual: Dark Country & Southern Gothic Songs of the Haunted South delivers exactly that.

Part of the Dark Country Boy Catalog

Dark Country Boy has released over 70 albums, making this one of the most extensive dark country and blues catalogs in independent music. Swamp Ritual: Dark Country & Southern Gothic Songs of the Haunted South is one entry in a body of work that spans Southern Gothic storytelling, Delta blues tradition, and outlaw country rebellion.
