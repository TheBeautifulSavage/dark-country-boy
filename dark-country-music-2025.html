Dark Country Music in 2025

Dark country music in 2025 is in one of its most creatively fertile periods. The genre has escaped the alt-country label that constrained it in the 90s and early 2000s and is now simply making dark, honest, rootsy American music without apology.

The State of Dark Country in 2025

2025 sees dark country music pulling from multiple directions simultaneously. The murder ballad revival continues, with artists like Amigo the Devil and Hank Williams III influencing a new generation of dark country songwriters. Gothic Americana has expanded from its Southern roots to encompass artists from the Pacific Northwest, the Midwest, and beyond.

Streaming has democratized dark country music in ways that benefit both artists and fans. Artists can build global audiences without label support; fans can discover obscure dark country records that would have been impossible to find a decade ago.
