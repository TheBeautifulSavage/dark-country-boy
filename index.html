About Dark Country Boy

Dark Country Boy is the sound of real life run through a guitar amp and set on fire. Drawing from Delta blues roots, outlaw country tradition, and the kind of hard-earned truth that doesn't fit in three minutes, this music exists in the spaces mainstream country forgot about.

The debut album Fire in the Blood (Dark Blues & Dark Country) is ten tracks of unfiltered American roots music — raw, gritty, and unapologetically real. No studio gloss. No radio compromises. Just a guitar, some stories, and a fire that doesn't go out.

Dark Country Boy sits in the tradition of Waylon Jennings, Johnny Cash, and the Handsome Family — artists who understood that real country music is about telling the truth, even when it burns.

"The blues has always been about truth. This is mine."
— Dark Country Boy

Genre: Dark Country • Outlaw Blues • Americana • Southern Gothic • Roots Music
