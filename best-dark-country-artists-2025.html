The Best Dark Country Artists to Follow in 2025

2025 is a strong year for dark country music. The genre is healthier than it's been in decades, with established artists releasing essential work and new voices emerging from unexpected places. Here's who to follow.

Established Dark Country Masters

Nick Cave & The Bad Seeds — The gold standard. Every release rewards deep listening. Tom Waits — Active and still operating at his own rules. Hank Williams III — The torch-bearer for outlaw dark country. Gillian Welch and David Rawlings — The duo continues to release devastating dark Americana.

Artists Rising in 2025

Amigo the Devil (Danny Kiranos) — Built a massive underground following with murder ballads and dark folk. His 2025 trajectory is essential to watch. S.G. Goodman — Kentucky-born, uncompromising dark country with sharp political edges. Caitlin Rose — Returning to her dark country roots. Tyler Childers — Not strictly dark country, but his Appalachian roots and raw honesty make him adjacent and essential.

Dark Country Boy: 70 Albums in 2025

Dark Country Boy is among the most prolific dark country artists working today — 70 albums, 1,400+ songs built in the gothic country and dark Americana tradition. A veteran combat paratrooper turned jiu-jitsu academy owner, Dark Country Boy brings life experience to music that the genre rarely sees.
