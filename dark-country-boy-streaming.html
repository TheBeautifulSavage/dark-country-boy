About Dark Country Boy's Sound

Dark Country Boy creates music at the intersection of dark blues, swamp blues, outlaw country, and Southern Gothic Americana. Drawing from the Delta blues tradition — Robert Johnson, Howlin' Wolf, Muddy Waters — and the outlaw country lineage of Johnny Cash and Waylon Jennings, the music lives in the spaces where these traditions meet.

The catalog includes 70 albums spanning dark and moody blues, Southern Gothic storytelling, hard outlaw country, and apocalyptic Americana. If you're searching for dark blues music, swamp blues guitar, gothic country, or just raw American music that doesn't apologize for being dark — this is it.
